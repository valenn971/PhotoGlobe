US

PhotoGlobe is about a space, a place where I can talk about what I like about traveling and knowing, where I can leave some information about what I love, history, culture, it is a place where those who have not traveled can teach about some of the cities that I write about, and at the same time learn from the cultural data that I leave in the pages, and if you have traveled, you can read some experiences from my blog, see photos, videos, and perhaps relive a little of the nostalgia that some places leave.

THE BEGINNING

The idea of sharing experiences and images always seduced me, but specifically, PhotoGlobe as something real, was born in 2018, after I made my first trip with friends, completely alone, without hiring a company of any kind, launching ourselves to know, explore and make a mistake, in mid-2018 I want to start sharing images and moments, but the idea of using my personal networks did not appeal to me, so I decided to create a page where I can show everything without shame, that's where PhotoGlobe was born.

VALENTIN

I am Valentin, the creator of the page, protagonist or co-protagonist of the experiences that I tell on the blog, and editor of the rest of the pages where you can find photos, videos, and data and history of the places, I was born on January 29 from 1997 and currently in 2023 I am 23 years old, and I have a kind of fascination for traveling and above all for getting to know cities and cultures, I have always had a childhood in which I traveled a lot and I think I always liked the idea of knowing new places, So a few years ago I decided to open my own space to share experiences and images of the places I have visited and that have left me most amazed.
